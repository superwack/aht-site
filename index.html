about us

Advanced Healthcare Technology, LLC was founded in 2003 by Dulce Villa, RN, after her own experience in the operating room. She and her staff consistently noted the gaps in first-hand expertise of their previously hired janitorial staff, a true risk for transmission of infection, leaving their patients and staff vulnerable to illness on a daily basis. Her goal from the very beginning has always been to provide top-tier commercial and janitorial services to healthcare business to ensure the best experience and safety for not just their own patients, but to patients across the tri-state area.

As a family-owned company, we pride ourselves in our ability to make our clients feel like an extended part of our family, where their needs are heard, preferences are met, and the expectations of the staff and patients are exceeded day after day.

Additional Services

Beyond just our customized janitorial plans unique to your site, we offer add-on services - either one-time (ad hoc) or monthly recurring services - so all your needs are met.

Floor Buffing and Waxing

Use of state-of-the-art buffer machines to deep clean ceramic tile flooring by stripping away dirt, stains and ground-in debris. Once the additional debris is mopped away with germicidal detergent, we finish off with a layer of wax coating to protect your floors and provide greater luster.

Window Cleaning

With the day-to-day of patients and staff - in addition to the ever-changing weather, dirty windows are inevitable. Smudges, marks, fingerprints and build-up on interior and exterior windows can be cleaned away to allow you to see brighter through clean windows.

Chemical-Free (Natural) Cleaning

As of 2019, we now also offer the use of pure essential oil-based (Young Living) cleaning products - for either all, or just a small portion of your clinic - for our more naturally-minded clients that prefer to opt away from harsh chemicals that believe in the 'power of green to clean'. This can be customized to your business' needs as an add-on with your contract of service.
